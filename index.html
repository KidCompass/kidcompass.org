Frequently asked questions

Can both parents see the same calendar?

Yes. On the co parent plan all linked adults see the same calendar and updates in real time.

Do I need the co parent plan if I am the only user?

No. The one dollar plan is enough if you are the only person who needs access to the account. You can upgrade later if you invite others.

How do I cancel?

You can cancel from your account settings at any time. Your plan will stay active until the end of the current billing period.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. It is used only to run Kid Compass and is never sold to advertisers.
